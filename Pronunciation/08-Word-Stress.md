# Word Stress


Native German words often stress the first root syllable: `ARbeiten`, `WOHnung`. In separable verbs, the prefix is stressed: `AUFstehen`, `EINkaufen`. Many international words stress a later syllable: Informa`TION`.

Learn stress with the word. Mark the stressed syllable in capitals during practice, then remove the visual support.
