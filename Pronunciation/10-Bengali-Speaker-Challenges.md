# Pronunciation Challenges for Bengali Speakers


Common areas to monitor:

1. Distinguishing long and short vowels.
2. Producing rounded front vowels `ö` and `ü`.
3. Avoiding an added vowel inside clusters such as `Sprache` or after final consonants.
4. Producing German `w` as /v/ and `z` as /ts/.
5. Distinguishing the two `ch` sounds.
6. Reducing unstressed `-en` naturally without deleting it completely.
7. Using German word stress rather than carrying Bengali rhythm unchanged.

Your accent is not a defect. Work on features that affect understanding, one at a time.
