# Sentence Stress and Intonation


Content words usually carry more stress than articles and pronouns. A neutral statement normally falls at the end. Yes/no questions often rise; W-questions often fall.

Compare:

- `Du kommst aus BERLIN.`
- `Kommst du aus BERLIN?`
- `WOHER kommst du?`

Copy whole melody during shadowing. German may sound less syllable-even than Bengali; do not give every word identical emphasis.
