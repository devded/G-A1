# IPA for Beginners


IPA is a map of sounds, not a second language to memorise. Use it only to distinguish features that ordinary spelling hides.

Useful symbols include `/iː/` long **i**, `/ɪ/` short **i**, `/yː/` long **ü**, `/ç/` the `ich` sound, `/x/` the `ach` sound, `/ʃ/` **sch**, and `/ts/` **z**.

Slashes show a phonemic transcription. Native audio remains the final reference. Compare one sound at a time and avoid reading IPA as English spelling.
