# Shadowing Guide


Use a 5–20 second clip.

1. Listen without text.
2. Listen with the transcript and mark stress.
3. Pause after each phrase and repeat.
4. Speak almost simultaneously with the recording.
5. Record yourself without the model.
6. Compare one feature: sound, stress, rhythm or intonation.
7. Repeat while focusing on meaning.

Five accurate minutes beats thirty distracted minutes. Use each weekly listening track as the main shadowing material.


## Practice checklist

- [ ] I listened before imitating.
- [ ] I said the examples aloud.
- [ ] I recorded at least one phrase.
- [ ] I identified one specific difference.
- [ ] I repeated the improved version.
