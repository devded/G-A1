# Goethe-Zertifikat A1 Guide

The examination assesses Listening (`Hören`), Reading (`Lesen`), Writing (`Schreiben`) and Speaking (`Sprechen`). Official Goethe information and model exercises should be treated as the current source of truth.

## Common official timing published for the adult A1 exam

| Module | Approximate duration |
|---|---:|
| Listening | 20 minutes |
| Reading | 25 minutes |
| Writing | 20 minutes |
| Speaking | 15 minutes |

Official practice: https://www.goethe.de/en/spr/prf/ueb/pa1.html

## Skill strategy

### Listening
Read the item first, predict the answer type, listen through distractors, and continue after missing a word.

### Reading
Identify text type and purpose; scan for the exact information required rather than translating every line.

### Writing
Complete every content point using short controlled sentences. Check greeting, closing, verb position and noun capitals.

### Speaking
Introduce yourself, ask/answer familiar questions, make/respond to requests, and show interaction rather than reciting only a memorised speech.

Use official model sets in Week 8 and the original repository mocks for additional practice.
