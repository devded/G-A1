# Eight-Week Exam Preparation Plan

## Week 1
- Main action: learn instructions, spelling and personal data.
- Milestone: Introduce yourself, spell personal information and understand another beginner’s introduction.
- Save one speaking and one writing sample.

## Week 2
- Main action: build profile/form accuracy.
- Milestone: Describe family, work or study, possessions and a simple living space.
- Save one speaking and one writing sample.

## Week 3
- Main action: train times, schedules and invitations.
- Milestone: Tell time, describe a daily routine and arrange a simple meeting.
- Save one speaking and one writing sample.

## Week 4
- Main action: train transaction details and requests.
- Milestone: Order food, shop for common items and express basic preferences.
- Save one speaking and one writing sample.

## Week 5
- Main action: train maps, notices and travel audio.
- Milestone: Ask for directions, understand basic travel information and complete a simple journey.
- Save one speaking and one writing sample.

## Week 6
- Main action: train descriptions and connected responses.
- Milestone: Describe people, places and weather, and discuss plans, abilities and obligations.
- Save one speaking and one writing sample.

## Week 7
- Main action: preview official tasks and repair weak areas.
- Milestone: Handle a simple health issue, appointment or emergency interaction.
- Save one speaking and one writing sample.

## Week 8
- Main action: complete official material plus two timed mocks.
- Milestone: Complete realistic Goethe A1 tasks confidently and independently.
- Save one speaking and one writing sample.
