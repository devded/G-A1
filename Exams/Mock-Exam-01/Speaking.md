# Speaking

Record the full simulation. Give your partner or AI one prompt at a time.
1. Name and spell your surname.
2. Where do you live and what do you do?
3. Ask your partner about weekend plans.
4. Request a glass of water politely.
5. Respond when a partner asks to borrow your pen.

## Evaluation

Score clarity, task completion, interaction, grammar/vocabulary control and pronunciation from 0–2 each. Ask for repetition naturally when needed.
