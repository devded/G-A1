# ÖSD Zertifikat A1 Guide

Official page: https://www.osd.at/en/exams/oesd-exams/oesd-zertifikat-a1-za1/

ÖSD A1 assesses elementary German for familiar everyday situations. Consult the official page for the current module structure, sample material, scoring and centre requirements.

## Preparation focus

- understand essential information in short audio/texts
- complete simple forms and messages
- provide personal information
- ask questions and make requests
- remain understandable across standard German varieties

Use the repository's broad listening voices and repair phrases, then complete current official ÖSD model material before examination day.
