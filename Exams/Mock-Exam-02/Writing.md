# Writing

## Task 1 — Form

Complete a fictional registration form with first name, surname, date/place of birth, address, phone, email, native language and signature.

## Task 2 — Message

A friend invited you to dinner. Write: thank them, say whether you can come, state your arrival time, and offer to bring something.

Write approximately 30–60 words. Include every content point, an appropriate greeting and closing, and clear A1 sentences.
