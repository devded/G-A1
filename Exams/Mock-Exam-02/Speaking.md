# Speaking

Record the full simulation. Give your partner or AI one prompt at a time.
1. Introduce yourself in six sentences.
2. Ask your partner where they come from.
3. Ask the price of a train ticket.
4. Request repetition and slower speech.
5. Invite your partner to do an activity and negotiate a time.

## Evaluation

Score clarity, task completion, interaction, grammar/vocabulary control and pronunciation from 0–2 each. Ask for repetition naturally when needed.
