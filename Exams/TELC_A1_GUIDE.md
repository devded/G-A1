# telc Deutsch A1 Guide

Official page: https://www.telc.net/en/language-examinations/certificate-exams/german/telc-german-a1/

The test assesses basic receptive and productive everyday German. Exact format, timings, scoring and available model tests should be checked on the official telc page and with the chosen centre because administrative details can change.

## Preparation focus

- short announcements and conversations
- signs, notices and simple messages
- forms and brief writing
- personal information and partner interaction
- reliable vocabulary for shopping, appointments, home, work and travel

The language developed in this repository transfers well, but complete at least one current official telc model test before choosing that exam.
