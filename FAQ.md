# Frequently Asked Questions

## Can I really reach A1 in eight weeks?

The schedule provides 112 guided hours, which is an intensive beginner programme. Results depend on consistency, active practice and prior language-learning experience. Completion prepares you for A1 tasks but does not guarantee an examination score.

## Must I memorise all 950 items?

No. About three quarters are marked active targets; the rest are recognition targets. Review high-frequency items more aggressively and understand the recognition set in context.

## Why are Bengali meanings included if English is the teaching language?

Bengali support reduces unnecessary ambiguity for a Bengali-speaking beginner. Grammar explanations remain mainly English so the learner can access wider German-learning terminology and resources.

## Do I need Anki?

No. CSV decks are included, but paper cards or another spaced-repetition tool work. Active recall and scheduled review matter more than the app.

## The audio sounds robotic. Is that a problem?

The local audio is generated with German text-to-speech so every lesson is self-contained. Use it for first-pass practice, dictation and shadowing. Assigned DW, Goethe, Easy German and Forvo resources provide more natural voices.

## Should I learn IPA?

Only enough to interpret difficult sounds. Each vocabulary item includes generated IPA as a pronunciation aid, while the pronunciation guides explain the symbols actually needed at A1.

## What happens if I fail a weekly quiz?

Use `Revision.md` to repair the lowest categories. Do not repeat the entire week unless most prerequisites are missing.

## Can I skip speaking because I have no partner?

No. Use self-questioning, role switching, timed recordings, picture description and the AI practice guide.

## Should I translate every sentence?

No. Translate during controlled practice, then move toward understanding complete German patterns. Avoid mechanical word-for-word translation.

## Why is basic Perfekt included?

A1 learners often need to understand or produce very simple statements about what happened. The course limits this to high-frequency survival patterns and does not teach advanced narration.

## Can I take days off?

The plan uses 56 consecutive study days for clarity. You may insert rest days, but continue in lesson order and keep the eight-week target only if your calendar still permits the full workload.

## Which exam is the main target?

Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1. The repository also explains telc Deutsch A1 and ÖSD Zertifikat A1.
