# 12-Week A1 German Curriculum Roadmap

> **Stress-Free Daily Pacing:** 35 Minutes per Day | 84 Days Total | 12 Weeks

---

## Full 12-Week Module Overview

### Phase 1: Foundations & Personal Identity (Weeks 1 - 3)
- **Week 1 (Days 01-07):** German Sounds, Alphabet, Pronunciation & Basic Greetings
- **Week 2 (Days 08-14):** Personal Info, Nationalities, Languages & Identity
- **Week 3 (Days 15-21):** Family Members, Relationships & Possessive Determiners

### Phase 2: Everyday Life & Home (Weeks 4 - 6)
- **Week 4 (Days 22-28):** House, Apartment Rooms, Furniture & Plural Nouns
- **Week 5 (Days 29-35):** Professions, Workplace, Numbers 0-100 & Age
- **Week 6 (Days 36-42):** Clock Time, Schedules, Routine & Separable Verbs

### Phase 3: Activities, Food & Shopping (Weeks 7 - 9)
- **Week 7 (Days 43-49):** Hobbies, Free Time, Sports & Sentence Word Order
- **Week 8 (Days 50-56):** Food, Beverages, Grocery Shopping & Accusative Case
- **Week 9 (Days 57-63):** Restaurants, Ordering Food & Expressing Preferences

### Phase 4: Environment, Health & Exam Readiness (Weeks 10 - 12)
- **Week 10 (Days 64-70):** City Places, Asking Directions & Basic Dative Case
- **Week 11 (Days 71-77):** Weather, Nature, Clothing & Modal Verbs
- **Week 12 (Days 78-84):** Health, Doctor Visit, Perfekt Tense & Mock Exams

---

## Daily 35-Minute Workflow

1. **7 Mins:** Listen to 3 daily vocabulary words with audio buttons
2. **10 Mins:** Read 1 simple grammar bite & worked example with translation & phonetics
3. **15 Mins:** Complete short 2-question fill-in practice
4. **3 Mins:** Check 3 boxes on daily self-check
