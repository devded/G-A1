# Exam Day Checklist

- [ ] Confirm venue, date, arrival time and identification requirements with the official test centre.
- [ ] Sleep and eat normally.
- [ ] Bring permitted identification and materials.
- [ ] Read every instruction and example.
- [ ] Listen for the required information type.
- [ ] Do not freeze after an unknown word.
- [ ] Include every writing content point.
- [ ] Speak clearly and interact with the partner/examiner.
- [ ] Ask for repetition politely when appropriate.
- [ ] Use short reliable A1 sentences.

Official administrative rules can change; verify them with your registered examination centre.
