# Vocabulary Revision Schedule

## Spaced-repetition cycle

| Stage | When | Required action |
|---|---|---|
| Learn | First exposure | Sound, meaning, article/plural, example |
| R0 | Same day | Recall German from meaning; say aloud |
| R1 | +1 day | Mixed recognition and production |
| R2 | +3 days | Sentence completion and dictation |
| R3 | +7 days | Weekly quiz and speaking reuse |
| R4 | +14 days | Cumulative production review |
| R5 | +30 days | Recognition audit and weak-card repair |
| Final | Week 8 | Full active/recognition classification |

## Honest card rating

- **Again:** could not retrieve it.
- **Hard:** retrieved slowly or missed article/plural.
- **Good:** correct with normal effort.
- **Easy:** immediate and stable in a sentence.

## Weekly audit

Sample 30 items randomly. Mark each as recognition-only, active with help, or active independently. Reduce new cards if essential recognition falls below 80%.
