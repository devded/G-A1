# Dictionaries and Translation Tools

| Tool | Best use | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| [LEO](https://dict.leo.org/german-english/) | bilingual meanings, examples and phrase lookup | choosing the first translation without context |
| [Duden](https://www.duden.de/) | spelling, article, plural and German definition | using dense definitions too early |
| [Forvo](https://forvo.com/languages/de/) | word pronunciation | assuming one recording is the only accepted accent |
| [DeepL](https://www.deepl.com/translator) | compare your completed sentence | copying a whole task before trying |
| [Google Translate](https://translate.google.com/) | emergency gist, camera or speech support | treating output as a grammar explanation |

## Verification checklist

For nouns verify article and plural. For verbs verify infinitive, irregular forms, required preposition/case and a natural example. Translation tools should be the last check, not the first author.
