# Core Resources

| Name / link | Purpose | Access | When | Advantages and limitations |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Goethe-Institut A1 practice](https://www.goethe.de/en/spr/prf/ueb/pa1.html) | Official model exercises, audio and vocabulary material | Free | Weeks 7–8 | Highest exam alignment; not a full day-by-day beginner course |
| [DW Nicos Weg](https://learngerman.dw.com/en/nicos-weg/c-36519789) | Graded video course and interactive practice | Free | Assigned A1 episodes throughout | Strong contextual sequence; follow course assignments instead of browsing randomly |
| [Easy German](https://www.easygerman.org/) | Natural spoken German with learner support | Free videos; optional membership | Selected Super Easy/Slow Easy content | Authentic voices; some content exceeds A1 |
| [Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net/) | Active-recall and spaced-repetition flashcards | Free desktop; platform availability differs | Daily | Powerful scheduling; card design still matters |
| [Forvo German](https://forvo.com/languages/de/) | Native-speaker pronunciation lookup | Free reference | As needed | Multiple speakers; recording quality and accent vary |
| [LEO Dictionary](https://dict.leo.org/german-english/) | Bilingual lookup, examples and forums | Free | As needed | Many translations require context selection |
| [Duden](https://www.duden.de/) | Spelling, gender and German definitions | Free reference with additional products | From Week 5 | Authoritative German reference; monolingual text may be difficult early |
| [DeepL](https://www.deepl.com/translator) | Translation comparison after independent work | Freemium | Limited checking | Can produce advanced phrasing without teaching the rule |
| [Language Reactor](https://www.languagereactor.com/) | Subtitle and replay support for compatible video | Freemium | Optional from Week 4 | Features and supported platforms can change |

## Selection rule

Use an external resource only for a defined task: a specific episode, pronunciation check, official practice section or timed activity. More resources do not automatically produce more learning.
