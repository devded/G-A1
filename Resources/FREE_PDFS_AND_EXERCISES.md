# Free PDFs and Exercise Sources

## Official Goethe materials

[Goethe A1 practice materials](https://www.goethe.de/en/spr/prf/ueb/pa1.html) include official practice for the four skills and vocabulary material. Use them in the final two weeks and follow the current site's instructions for downloads/audio.

## telc

[telc Deutsch A1](https://www.telc.net/en/language-examinations/certificate-exams/german/telc-german-a1/) provides official exam information and available practice links.

## ÖSD

[ÖSD Zertifikat A1](https://www.osd.at/en/exams/oesd-exams/oesd-zertifikat-a1-za1/) provides official format information and available model material.

## DW

[DW Learn German](https://learngerman.dw.com/) provides free interactive exercises around Nicos Weg and other courses.

Always download only from official or clearly licensed sources. The original mock exams in this repository are newly written and do not reproduce official papers.
