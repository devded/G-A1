# Apps and Flashcards

| App | Role | Access | Course recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net/) | spaced review | free desktop; platform differences | primary flashcard tool using repository CSVs |
| [Memrise](https://www.memrise.com/) | optional phrase exposure | freemium | maximum two short sessions/week; do not replace course sequence |
| [LingQ](https://www.lingq.com/) | optional reading/listening library | freemium | optional Weeks 6–8 for easy input |
| [Language Reactor](https://www.languagereactor.com/) | video subtitle control | freemium | optional replay support from Week 4 |

Do not maintain four competing vocabulary systems. Choose Anki or paper cards as the primary review system.
