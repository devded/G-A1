# Pronunciation Resources

- [Forvo German](https://forvo.com/languages/de/) — check individual words across native speakers. Compare more than one recording when available.
- [Duden](https://www.duden.de/) — confirm spelling, stress information and available pronunciation support.
- [DW Learn German](https://learngerman.dw.com/) — imitate complete beginner sentences in context.
- [Easy German](https://www.easygerman.org/) — observe natural rhythm, regional variation and everyday pronunciation.
- Local course WAV files — repeat exact weekly dialogues offline.

## Recommended sequence

1. Hear the complete phrase.
2. Check a difficult word.
3. Return to the phrase.
4. Shadow it.
5. Record yourself.
6. Compare one feature only.

Avoid building pronunciation entirely from Bengali or English transliteration.
