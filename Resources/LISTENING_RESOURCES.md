# Listening Resources

| Resource | Purpose | Difficulty | Recommended use | Advantage | Limitation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| [DW Nicos Weg](https://learngerman.dw.com/en/nicos-weg/c-36519789) | graded story and exercises | A1 upward | 2–3 assigned segments/week | context and repetition | interface is content-rich; stay with assignments |
| [Easy German Super Easy](https://www.easygerman.org/) | slow natural street/dialogue input | beginner | 1 selected clip/week | real voices and subtitles | vocabulary may extend beyond the week |
| [Slow German](https://slowgerman.com/) | carefully spoken topics | mixed | optional from Week 6 | clear delivery | many episodes are beyond A1 |
| [Goethe A1 practice](https://www.goethe.de/en/spr/prf/ueb/pa1.html) | exam-like listening | A1 assessment | Weeks 7–8 | official alignment | better for testing than first teaching |
| Course WAV tracks | exact weekly language | controlled A1 | daily | offline and aligned | synthetic voice is not a replacement for human variety |

## Weekly approach

Use course audio first, one human graded source second, and official exam audio near the end. Repeat short input deeply rather than collecting many unfinished playlists.
