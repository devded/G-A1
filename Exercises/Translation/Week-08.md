# Week 8 Controlled Translation

Translate into German using only familiar structures.

1. My name is ... and I come from ...
2. Today I study German for two hours.
3. I do not have time in the morning.
4. Where is the station?
5. I would like a tea, please.
6. Can you repeat that slowly?
7. We meet on Saturday at six o'clock.
8. The room is small but beautiful.

Then translate three of your German answers naturally into Bengali and compare meaning rather than word order.
