# Week 6 Mixed Revision

## 30-minute checkpoint

1. Recall 15 random weekly words — 5 min.
2. Build five sentences from weekly grammar — 5 min.
3. Listen to one daily track and extract details — 5 min.
4. Read the weekly text and answer three questions — 5 min.
5. Speak for one minute — 5 min.
6. Write a four-sentence message — 5 min.

Mark each area: Independent / With help / Needs review. Repair only the two weakest areas before repeating.
