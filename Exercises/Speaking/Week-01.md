# Week 1 Speaking Practice

**Milestone:** Introduce yourself, spell personal information and understand another beginner’s introduction.

## Prompt ladder

1. Give five individual answers.
2. Connect the answers for 30 seconds.
3. Speak for the weekly target with keywords only.
4. Perform one question-and-answer exchange.
5. Change one key detail unexpectedly.
6. Use one repair phrase.

Useful patterns: `Ich heiße …`, `Ich bin … Jahre alt.`, `Ich komme aus …`, `Ich wohne in …`, `Ich spreche …`, `Wie heißen Sie?`, `Woher kommst du?`
