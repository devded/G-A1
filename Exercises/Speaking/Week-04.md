# Week 4 Speaking Practice

**Milestone:** Order food, shop for common items and express basic preferences.

## Prompt ladder

1. Give five individual answers.
2. Connect the answers for 30 seconds.
3. Speak for the weekly target with keywords only.
4. Perform one question-and-answer exchange.
5. Change one key detail unexpectedly.
6. Use one repair phrase.

Useful patterns: `Ich möchte …`, `Ich kaufe einen/eine/ein …`, `Was kostet …?`, `Haben Sie …?`, `Ich habe kein/keine …`, `Die Rechnung, bitte.`
