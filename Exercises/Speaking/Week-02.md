# Week 2 Speaking Practice

**Milestone:** Describe family, work or study, possessions and a simple living space.

## Prompt ladder

1. Give five individual answers.
2. Connect the answers for 30 seconds.
3. Speak for the weekly target with keywords only.
4. Perform one question-and-answer exchange.
5. Change one key detail unexpectedly.
6. Use one repair phrase.

Useful patterns: `Ich habe …`, `Das ist mein/meine …`, `Er/Sie arbeitet als …`, `Wir wohnen in …`, `Die Wohnung hat …`, `Im Zimmer steht …`
