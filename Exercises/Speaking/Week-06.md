# Week 6 Speaking Practice

**Milestone:** Describe people, places and weather, and discuss plans, abilities and obligations.

## Prompt ladder

1. Give five individual answers.
2. Connect the answers for 30 seconds.
3. Speak for the weekly target with keywords only.
4. Perform one question-and-answer exchange.
5. Change one key detail unexpectedly.
6. Use one repair phrase.

Useful patterns: `Es ist …`, `Heute regnet/schneit es.`, `Ich kann/muss/will/darf …`, `… und/aber/oder/denn …`, `A ist größer als B.`, `Ich … lieber …`
