# Picture Description

Use the safe sequence:

1. `Auf dem Bild sehe ich ...`
2. State place: room, street, park, shop.
3. Name people and objects.
4. Describe positions with familiar chunks.
5. Describe actions in the present tense.
6. Add weather, colors or simple opinion.

## Prompt

Imagine a family in a park. Two children play football, the mother reads, the father drinks coffee, a dog sits near a tree, and the weather is sunny.

Speak for 60–90 seconds. Do not invent advanced detail. Use short connected sentences with `und`, `aber`, `auch`.
