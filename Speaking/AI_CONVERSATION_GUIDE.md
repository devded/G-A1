# AI Conversation Guide

Use this prompt:

```text
Act as my German A1 conversation partner.
Use only CEFR A1 German and the current topic: [TOPIC].
Ask one short question at a time and wait for my answer.
Correct only important errors, explain briefly in English, and ask me to repeat the corrected sentence.
Do not answer for me and do not introduce advanced grammar.
After eight turns, give me three strengths, three priority corrections and a short retry task.
```

## Useful scenarios

- first meeting
- café
- supermarket
- clothing shop
- station
- hotel
- doctor
- phone appointment

AI feedback can be wrong. Compare disputed corrections with the repository patterns or a reliable dictionary.
