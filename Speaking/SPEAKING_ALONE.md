# Speaking Alone

## Seven methods

1. Answer your own questions.
2. Switch roles in a dialogue.
3. Narrate your current action.
4. Describe objects around you.
5. Speak from five keywords for one minute.
6. Record, transcribe, correct and re-record.
7. Give yourself five seconds to answer familiar questions.

## No-script ladder

- Round 1: full model.
- Round 2: sentence starters.
- Round 3: keywords only.
- Round 4: no notes.
- Round 5: unexpected follow-up question.
