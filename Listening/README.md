# A1 Listening Course

Listening progresses from sounds and numbers to dialogues, announcements and exam tasks. Each weekly lesson links to locally generated WAV audio and a transcript hidden below the task.

## Three-pass method

1. **Gist:** identify speaker, place and topic.
2. **Details:** collect names, numbers, times, prices, places and actions.
3. **Confirmation:** use the transcript only after two genuine attempts.

Never begin with the transcript. Missing one word is not failure; keep listening for the information required by the question.
