# Listening Roadmap

| Week | Input | Main extraction skill | Checkpoint |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | letters, greetings, numbers | names and personal data | introduction |
| 2 | family and home descriptions | relationships and objects | profile matching |
| 3 | schedules and routines | times and sequence | daily routine |
| 4 | restaurant and shop dialogue | item, quantity and price | order form |
| 5 | directions and transport | route, time, platform | journey task |
| 6 | weather and plans | description and intention | forecast |
| 7 | calls, health and emergency | problem and required action | appointment |
| 8 | mixed exam-style recordings | distractor control and timing | two mock tests |

## Weekly minimum

Complete all seven local audio tasks, one short dictation, one shadowing recording and the weekly listening checkpoint.
