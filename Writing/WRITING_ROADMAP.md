# Writing Roadmap

| Week | Product | Length |
|---:|---|---:|
| 1 | personal form and profile | 4–6 sentences |
| 2 | family/home description | 6–8 sentences |
| 3 | routine and invitation | 8–10 sentences / short message |
| 4 | shopping list and reservation | practical note |
| 5 | directions and travel message | 50–70 words |
| 6 | description and plan | 60–80 words |
| 7 | appointment or absence message | 30–60 words |
| 8 | timed form and email | exam-style |

Write a first version, check one category at a time, then produce a clean second version.
