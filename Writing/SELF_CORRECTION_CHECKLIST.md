# Writing Self-Correction Checklist

## Content
- [ ] I answered every requested point.
- [ ] The reader can understand the purpose.

## Sentence structure
- [ ] Every sentence has a conjugated verb.
- [ ] The main-clause verb is in position 2.
- [ ] Modal/separable/perfect parts are in the expected final position.

## Words
- [ ] Nouns begin with capitals.
- [ ] Articles/cases match patterns I have learned.
- [ ] Verb endings match the subject.
- [ ] `nicht` and `kein` are used appropriately.

## Format
- [ ] Greeting and closing fit the situation.
- [ ] Dates, times and names are clear.
- [ ] I read the final text aloud.
