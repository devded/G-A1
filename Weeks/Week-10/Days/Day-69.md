## Step 2 вАФ Grammar Bite (10 min)

<div class="grammar-box">

### Impersonal Pronoun 'man' + Modal Verbs

### Using 'man' (people in general / one / you)

In German, **man** (spelled with ONE 'n', unlike *der Mann*) refers to people in general:

вАҐ **Man kann hier gut essen.** (One can eat well here / You can eat well here.)  
вАҐ **Man muss einen Helm tragen.** (One must wear a helmet.)  
вАҐ **Bei Regen sollte man eine Jacke anziehen.** (In rain one should put on a jacket.)

> <Icon name="target" /> **Conjugation Rule:**  
> *man* uses the exact same 3rd person singular verb form as **er / sie / es**!  
> вАҐ *man kann*, *man muss*, *man darf*, *man soll*, *man will*, *man mag*.
