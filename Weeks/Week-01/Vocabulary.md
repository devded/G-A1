# Week 1 Vocabulary — Sounds, Greetings and Identity

**Target:** 120 lexical items. Active items should be produced in speech or writing; recognition items should first be understood in context.

## Learning rule

- Nouns: article + singular + plural
- Verbs: infinitive + useful sentence
- Phrases: learn as one communication chunk
- Review: same day, +1, +3, +7, +14 and +30 days
