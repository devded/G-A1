## ūüďĖ Step 2 ‚ÄĒ Grammar Bite (10 min)

<div class="grammar-box">

### Paying & Tipping Etiquette in Germany

### How to Ask for the Bill

‚ÄĘ **Zahlen, bitte!** / **Wir m√∂chten bitte bezahlen.**  
‚ÄĘ **Die Rechnung, bitte!**

### Together or Separately?
Waiters in Germany will always ask: **Zusammen oder getrennt?**

‚ÄĘ **Getrennt, bitte.** = Each person pays for their own food.  
‚ÄĘ **Zusammen, bitte.** = One person pays the total bill.

### Tipping Culture in Germany (5‚Äď10%)

In Germany, you tell the waiter the **total amount including tip** when handing over cash!

‚ÄĘ Bill is 18,20 ‚ā¨ ‚Üí Hand over 20 ‚ā¨ bill and say: **"20 Euro, bitte!"** (or **"Stimmt so!"**)  
‚ÄĘ The waiter will give you back 0 ‚ā¨ change (tipping ~1.80 ‚ā¨).
