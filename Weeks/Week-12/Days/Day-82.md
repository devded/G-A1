### Goethe A1 Speaking Part 3: Polite Requests & Reactions

### Sprechen Teil 3 ‚ÄĒ Bittgesuche (Requests & Action Cards)

In Sprechen Teil 3, you draw an **picture object card** (e.g. a picture of an apple, a pen, a CD, a glass of water).  
You must formulate a **polite request / command** to your partner, and your partner must react!

1. **Geben Sie mir bitte [Item + Accusative]!**
