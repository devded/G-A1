### Goethe A1 Writing (Schreiben) Structure & Formula

### Schreiben Exam Overview (approx. 20 minutes)

The A1 Writing exam has **2 Parts (Teile)**:

1. **Teil 1 (Form Filling - 5 points):** Fill in 5 missing pieces of information on a form based on a short text (e.g. name, number of persons, arrival date, payment method).
2. **Teil 2 (Short Message - approx. 30 words):** Write a short email/SMS covering **all 3 prompt points**!

### ūüéĮ The Perfect A1 Short Message Template

```
Liebe(r) [Name], (or: Sehr geehrte Damen und Herren,)

[Point 1: Reason for writing]
Ich schreibe Ihnen, weil... / Ich m√∂chte...

[Point 2: Information / Request]
K√∂nnen wir... ? / Ich komme am...

[Point 3: Asking for confirmation]
Bitte antworten Sie mir! / Passt das?

Viele Gr√ľ√üe / Mit freundlichen Gr√ľ√üen,
[Your Name]
```

> ‚ö†ÔłŹ **Critical Rule:** You MUST cover **ALL 3 prompt points** to get full points!
