# Week 5 Revision Plan

## Daily review queue

- Same day: today's new items and one sentence pattern
- +1 day: full active recall
- +3 days: production cards and one dictation
- +7 days: weekly quiz sample
- +14 days: mixed old/new review
- +30 days: recognition audit and speaking reuse

## Targeted recovery

### Vocabulary below 80%

Reduce new cards temporarily. Review 20 difficult items through article recall, sound, example and personal sentence.

### Grammar below 70%

Return to the relevant daily grammar section. Write the purpose, structure, two examples and one corrected mistake from memory.

### Listening difficulty

Use a 10–20 second segment: listen, identify keywords, open transcript, close transcript, dictate, shadow.

### Speaking difficulty

Use a keyword list rather than a full script. Record 30 seconds, then 45, then the weekly target.

### Writing difficulty

Rewrite using shorter sentences. Check one category at a time: content, verbs, noun capitals, articles, spelling.

## Milestone retest

> Ask for directions, understand basic travel information and complete a simple journey.

Retest after the recovery activities. Mark “Can Do Independently” only without a complete model answer.
