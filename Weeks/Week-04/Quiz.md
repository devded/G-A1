# Week 4 Quiz

**Time:** 45–60 minutes · **Pass target:** 70% plus completion of speaking and writing

1. Vocabulary production: write eight assigned items from English prompts.
2. Article and plural: give four complete noun packages.
3. Grammar: complete five target structures.
4. Word order: correct three sentences.
5. Listening: use Day 7 audio and record the topic plus two details.
6. Reading: answer the five questions for Passage 3.
7. Speaking: complete the weekly checkpoint recording.
8. Writing: complete the weekly prompt and correction checklist.

## Scoring

| Area | Points |
|---|---:|
| Vocabulary | 20 |
| Grammar and word order | 25 |
| Listening | 15 |
| Reading | 10 |
| Speaking | 15 |
| Writing | 15 |
| **Total** | **100** |

## Result interpretation

- 85–100: ready to continue; maintain normal review.
- 70–84: continue and repair the two lowest categories.
- 50–69: complete the targeted recovery plan in `Revision.md` before adding a full new vocabulary load.
- Below 50: review prerequisites and repeat the skill checks, not every page.

## Answer policy

Vocabulary and grammar answers are in `Exercises.md` and the daily answer sections. Listening and reading answers come from the transcripts and passages. Speaking and writing are graded with their rubrics.
