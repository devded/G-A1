# Week 4 Reading

Read once for purpose, then scan for names, numbers, times, locations and actions.


## Passage 1

> Café Morgenrot: Kaffee 2,50 Euro, Tee 2 Euro, Käsebrötchen 3 Euro, Suppe 5 Euro. Geöffnet Montag bis Samstag von 8 bis 18 Uhr.

1. Who or what is the text mainly about?
2. Write two important details from the text.
3. Find one number, time, place or descriptive word.
4. Copy one complete German sentence and translate its main meaning.
5. Which sentence pattern from today appears in the text?

<details><summary>Answer guidance</summary>

Answers must be supported by the text. For question 4, a natural English or Bengali meaning is accepted; do not copy German word order mechanically.

</details>
