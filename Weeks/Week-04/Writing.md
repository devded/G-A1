# Week 4 Writing

## Weekly outcomes

- shopping list
- food preference paragraph
- reservation message

## Progression tasks

1. Write five isolated words from memory with articles where needed.
2. Write five correct simple sentences.
3. Connect two sentences with `und`, and later with other known connectors.
4. Write one practical text connected to the weekly milestone.
5. Correct and rewrite the text.

## Weekly prompt

Write 40–60 words that demonstrate: **Order food, shop for common items and express basic preferences.**

Include:

- all required personal or practical information
- at least eight weekly vocabulary items
- at least two target grammar patterns
- one question, request or response where suitable
- greeting and closing if the task is a message

## Correction checklist

- [ ] Every sentence has a conjugated verb.
- [ ] The finite verb is in the correct main-clause position.
- [ ] Nouns begin with capital letters.
- [ ] Articles match the known gender and case.
- [ ] Required content points are present.
- [ ] The final version is rewritten, not merely annotated.
