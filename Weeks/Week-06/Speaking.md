# Week 6 Speaking

## Weekly outcomes

- weather report
- picture description
- weekend plans
- comparisons

## Daily five-minute drill

1. Answer three familiar questions.
2. Ask the same questions as both `du` and formal `Sie` where appropriate.
3. Use one time, place, quantity or reason.
4. Repair one simulated misunderstanding.

## Role-plays

1. **Information exchange:** introduce the weekly topic and ask three questions.
2. **Practical task:** one person requests information or service; the other responds.
3. **Problem:** one detail is unavailable, wrong or unclear; negotiate a simple alternative.

## Recording checkpoint

Speak for 105 seconds on the weekly milestone. Use ten active words and three sentence patterns. Record twice, correcting only the three most important errors between attempts.

## Self-rating

| Criterion | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Task completion | not completed | partly | all required points |
| Understandability | mostly unclear | understandable with effort | generally clear |
| Vocabulary | isolated words | some phrases | useful range for topic |
| Grammar | meaning often blocked | errors but meaning clear | controlled A1 patterns |
| Interaction | no questions/response | limited | asks and responds |
