# Week 3 Pronunciation

## Goals

- separable-verb stress
- consonant clusters
- sp and st
- question intonation

## Daily routine

1. Select five assigned words containing the target feature.
2. Listen to the local audio and, when needed, Forvo.
3. Repeat slowly twice and naturally twice.
4. Put each word in a short sentence.
5. Record and compare one feature only.

## Bengali-speaker attention points

- Keep German long and short vowels distinct.
- Do not insert extra vowels inside consonant clusters.
- Do not replace `w` automatically with English /w/; German `w` is usually /v/.
- Treat `z` as /ts/ and `j` as /j/ like English “yes.”
- Learn `ö` and `ü` through lip position and audio, not Bengali spelling alone.
