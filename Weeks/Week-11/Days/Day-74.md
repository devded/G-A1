## Step 2 — Grammar Bite (10 min)

<div class="grammar-box">

### Imperative (Commands & Advice) for Instructions

### Imperative Formula (Polite Formal: Sie)

To give instructions, advice, or commands in German, **put the verb in Position 1 followed by Sie**!

<Icon name="arrow-right" /> **Verb (Infinitive) + Sie + ... !**

• **Nehmen Sie** *zwei Tabletten täglich.* (Take two tablets daily.)  
• **Trinken Sie** *viel Wasser.* (Drink plenty of water.)  
• **Bleiben Sie** *drei Tage im Bett.* (Stay in bed for three days.)  
• **Gehen Sie** *zum Arzt.* (Go to the doctor.)

### Preposition **nach** (after) & **vor** (before) + Dative
• **vor dem Essen** = before eating / before meals  
• **nach dem Essen** = after eating / after meals
